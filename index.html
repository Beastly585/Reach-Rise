Workflow

Most projects flow through these 5 steps
Step 1: Contact Form
01
Contact Form
Every project starts off with a consultation request.
Each project requires you to submit a contact form The more information, the better.
02
Consultation (free)
Once I receive the form, we'll schedule a time to meet and discuss:
  • Your goals in detail
  • Specifics of the project - timeline, deliverables, etc
  • Final quote and deposit terms
03
Drafting
The first stage of designing.
During this step I'll put together a basic layout of the final design, checking-in with you periodically to ensure the final product matches your vision.
04
Designing
Once the draft is finalized, I'll start working on details to finalize the design.
By this point, I'll have gotten a good idea of your vision, so check-ins will be weekly and require less of your time.
05
Final Handoff
Once the design is finalized, I'll provide you with all the documents you need to have full ownership of your website.
