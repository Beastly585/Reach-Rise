EXISTING WEBSITE

  • Web Design

    I'll re-design your website to make it easier to use while it smoothly guides the customer to your target

  • Re-Development

    I'll edit your website layout to reflect desired changes

  • Social Media Kit - I'll create or edit necessary media depending on your goals

ENGAGE

03

SEO - Search Engine Optimization

SEO optimizes a website to improve its ranking on search engines, driving organic traffic, which helps draw more customers to your site by making it more visible and relevant to their searches.

I offer the following SEO services:

  • On-Page SEO - Optimizing your websie pages by using proper element hierarchy, keywords, meta tags, internal linking, and content
  • Off-Page SEO - Backlinks, social media signals, guest posting, and other strategies to improve you site authority
  • Technical SEO - Technical aspects of your websites such as speed, mobile optimization, crawlability, and others to improve site performance and visibility
  • SEO Analytics - An audit of your site to determine opportunities to drive better customer visibility and website performance

MARKETING

Marketing is crucial to stay on top of, especially when you make changes to your products and services.

  • Online Marketing - Social media, email, and web development
  • Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) - A/B testing, UX analysis, and call-to-action optimization
  • Web Analytics & Tracking - Deep analysis of how your online presence fares on Google and how to drive more (targeted) traffic

MAINTAIN

04

ONGOING WEBSITE MAINTENANCE

A website requires regular updates and maintenance to stay functional, secure, and optimized. I offer ongoing support to ensure your website continues to serve your business and customers effectively:

  • Regular Software Updates - Keep your website's content management system (CMS), plugins, and security features up-to-date to avoid vulnerabilities.
  • Website Backups - Regular backups to ensure data security and allow for easy recovery in case of issues.
  • Content Updates - Update website text, images, and other media to keep your content fresh and relevant to your audience.
  • Bug Fixes - Resolve any technical issues or bugs that arise on the website to ensure optimal user experience.

SECURITY & PERFORMANCE

Maintaining a secure and high-performance website is essential for both user trust and SEO. I offer ongoing services to keep your site fast, secure, and always available:

  • Security Monitoring - Regular scans for malware, vulnerabilities, and potential security threats to protect your site.
  • Performance Optimization - Continuously optimize your website for faster load times and better user experience, including image compression, caching, and script minification.
  • SEO Audits & Adjustments - Regular SEO audits to ensure your website's search engine ranking stays high and make adjustments as necessary.
